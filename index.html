FascistValues

What is FascistValues?

FascistValues is a fascist quiz inspired by and based upon the 8values quiz that says you what of the many branches of the fascism you belong, considering fascism simply as an authoritarian, third-positionist ideology. This test is only for fascists, if you're not, this test isn't made for you. At the end of the question, you can watch your position respect several matters.

There are questions in the test.

What are the values?

There are currently seven axes, each of which has two opposing values. They are:

Left vs. Right
In the economic axis, the right usually puts more focus on private property and companies and more moderate changes on the capitalist economy, for example, the first years of the Mussolini's Italy. While the left usually will put more effort on the working class, encouraging the trade unions, and an almost total planification of the economy, like the National-Syndicalists or the Strasserist. All of these ones always inside the third positionist economy.

Totalitarian vs. Moderate
In the government axis, the totalitarians usually tend to establish and state what is above all, with a supreme leader who is object of cult and with infallible law enforcement, like the totalitarian regime in the Nazi Germany. The moderates, while also promoting a strong state, often agree with the freedom of speech and press, even sometimes they include systems of partial democracy as the organic democracy.

Race vs. Culture
In the identity axis, those who set above the race, they give it more importance, and their goals are often to unite all their peoples under the same state, they also usually opposed to the inmigration, for example, the Nazi Germany again. Those who set above the culture usually seek to preserve the national values and culture of their country, while are also opposed to multiculturalism, as they expect inmigrants to integrate, like the falangists in Spain.

Pacifist vs. Militarist
In the military axis, the pacifists usually want to keep peace to ensure the prosperity of their nation, they see a military conflict as an innecesary loss of both money and human lives, for example, the British Fascists of Oswald Mosley. The militarists, usually want to start a war to retake the "former glory" of their countries, they see the war as the only way to achieve their national goals, for example, the Mussolini's Italy.

Religion vs. Secular
In the religious axis, the religious often want to establish a strong religious identity based on the traditional religion, maybe with the reunification of church and state, like the clerical fascist. The secularist, want to remove the traditional religion away from the state, sometimes to establish a fully secular state, like the British fascists, and othertimes to establish a new pagan/esoteric religion, like the SchutStaffel(SS) in Germany.

Production vs. Nature
In the acceleration axis, those who set above the production, want to boster the productivity of their country at any coast, even if this causes damage to the nature. While those who set above the nature, want to keep nature at any coast, sometimes even wanting to go back to a pre-industrial life style, like the Eco-Fascists.

Reactionary vs. Progressive
In the social axis, the reactionaries tend to keep the traditions and hierarchies, they are strongly opposed to the modern course of the society, like the Integralists or the Clerical fascists. The progressives want to trigger some social changes, and one day they could be considered as revolutionaries, like the falangists and strasserists in their times, and the homofacism or the stransserism nowadays.

What does "Closest Match" mean in the results?

Similar to 8values, this quiz will attempt to match you with a specific leftist ideology. There are currently twelve possible ideologies, with more to come in the future. This is a work in progress and may not work as intended. Suggestions are very welcome. The current ideologies are: Italian Fascism, National-Socialism, British Fascism, Falangism, Esoteric Fascism, Integralism, Clerical Fascism, Eco-Fascism, Strasserism, Homofascism, Silver Legionism and Black Hundredism.

I don't like my scores!

Please remember that you are not intended to get a 100% score in any of the categories. The point of the quiz is to challenge your views. If you have any suggestions or constructive criticism please open an issue onthe GitHub Page.
